## What is 2FA?

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring:
1. **Your password** (something you know)
2. **Your phone** (something you have)

Even if someone steals your password, they cannot access your account without your phone.

## Setup Instructions (5 Minutes, One-Time)

### Step 1: Install Authenticator App

Download **one** of these free apps on your smartphone:
- **Google Authenticator** (recommended, free)
- **Microsoft Authenticator** (free)
- **Authy** (free, with backup features)

### Step 2: Enable 2FA in Your Portal

1. Log into your Owner Portal
2. Click the **üîê Security** tab
3. Click **"Enable 2FA"** button
4. A QR code will appear on screen

### Step 3: Scan QR Code

1. Open your authenticator app
2. Tap "Add Account" or "+"
3. Select "Scan QR Code"
4. Point camera at QR code on screen
5. Your app will show a 6-digit number

### Step 4: Verify and Activate

1. Enter the 6-digit number from your app
2. Click "Verify and Enable 2FA"
3. ‚úÖ Done! 2FA is now active

## Common Questions

**Q: What if I lose my phone?**  
A: Contact support immediately. We can verify your identity and reset 2FA.

**Q: What if the code doesn't work?**  
A: Codes expire every 30 seconds. Wait for a fresh code and try again. Ensure your phone's time is accurate.

**Q: Do I need internet on my phone?**  
A: No! The authenticator app works completely offline.

**Q: Can I disable 2FA?**  
A: Yes, from the Security tab. You'll need to enter your password to confirm.
