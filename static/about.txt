## What is EFRIS?
EFRIS (Electronic Fiscal Receipt and Invoice System) is Uganda Revenue Authority's electronic fiscalization system. All businesses in Uganda must submit tax invoices to EFRIS and receive fiscal document numbers (FDN) for tax compliance.
