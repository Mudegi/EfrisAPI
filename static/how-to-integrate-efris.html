What is EFRIS and Why Do You Need It?

EFRIS (Electronic Fiscal Receipt and Invoice System) is the Uganda Revenue Authority's mandatory electronic fiscalization system. As of 2024, all businesses in Uganda must submit their tax invoices to EFRIS and receive a Fiscal Document Number (FDN) before issuing invoices to customers.

If you're using QuickBooks, Xero, or Zoho Books for accounting, you need a way to automatically send your invoices from these systems to URA EFRIS without manual data entry.

The Challenge: Manual EFRIS Submission

Many Uganda businesses face these problems:

  • Creating invoices in QuickBooks/Xero/Zoho, then manually entering the same data into EFRIS portal
  • Wasting hours every day on duplicate data entry
  • Making errors that lead to URA penalties
  • Employees forgetting to fiscalize some invoices
  • No automatic backup of EFRIS submissions
